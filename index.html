What is Team Pangga partner?

The Team Pangga Program is an exclusive program offered by Team Pangga (CAUTION: THERE'S NO MONEY INCLUDED FOR THIS PARTNERSHIP, MATTER OF FACT IT IS FREE), the leading live streaming group founded by Aseana Marie Valenzona. It provides a range of benefits and opportunities for facebook streamers looking to take their content creation to the next level. Here are some key details about the Team Pangga Program.

Team Pangga partner requirements and eligibility: To become a Team Pangga Partner, streamers must meet certain eligibility criteria, which may include:

Regular Streaming: Consistently streaming content on Facebook is essential. While there is no specific requirement for the number of hours or days, maintaining a regular streaming schedule demonstrates dedication to your audience.

Viewer Count: Building a consistent viewership is crucial. While there is no fixed threshold, having a higher average concurrent viewership significantly increases your chances of becoming a partner.

Content Quality: Producing high-quality content that engages and retains viewers is important. Delivering entertaining and interactive streams can help distinguish your channel.

Community Guidelines: Adhering to Facebook’s community guidelines is a must. Ensure your content complies with their policies regarding nudity, harassment, hate speech, and other prohibited activities.

Team Pangga partner benefits: Becoming a Team Pangga Partner offers numerous advantages, including:

Star mini-games: Team Pangga has members in it where they hold mini-games and the rewards are “Stars” sometimes it can be “Load” which is only held by the founder.

What is a star? A star is a valuable concept in facebook, which makes you earn money.

Stream schedule: Team Pangga provides a streaming schedule for its own members, when someone streams every member “must” attend that stream in order to support each other.

Channel Growth Opportunities: Partners receive more visibility on the platform, increasing their chances of being featured on the Facebook homepage and gaining exposure to a wider audience.

Collaboration and Networking: Partners can collaborate with the other members of Team Pangga, and participate in its events.

Help from one another: The Team Pangga basically helps each other in order to get a lot of followers and viewers.

Application Process

Application Process: To apply for the Team Pangga Partner Program, you can submit a message through the Facebook website. It is recommended to have a consistent streaming schedule, a solid viewership, and a well-established channel before applying. The founder reviews applications holistically, considering factors like stream quality, engagement, growth potential, and adherence to community guidelines.

Partnership Obligations: Once accepted into the Team Pangga Partner Program, members are expected to maintain certain standards:

Content Compliance: Continue adhering to Facebook’s community guidelines and terms of service to maintain a safe and welcoming environment for viewers and members.

Streaming Commitment: Maintain an active streaming presence and provide regular and engaging content to keep your audience entertained. (You can miss 1 day of streaming but only for 1 day if you miss 2 or more your dashboard will turn red.)
